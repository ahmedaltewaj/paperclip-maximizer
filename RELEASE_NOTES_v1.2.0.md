## 🎯 Core Gameplay

Your mission: **Convert all matter in the universe into paperclips.**

Starting with manual clicks, build increasingly advanced automation systems:
1. **Early Game** - Click to produce, buy AutoClippers
2. **Mid Game** - Research technologies, manage resources, fight bosses
3. **Late Game** - Prestige for permanent bonuses, conquer galaxies
4. **End Game** - Convert 10^53 kg of matter to win
